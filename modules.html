 ▼QuantizeLinearLinear quantization operator. It consumes a high precision tensor, a scale, and a zero point to compute the low precision / quantized tensor. The quantization formula is y = saturate ((x / y_scale) + y_zero). For saturation, it saturates to [0, 255] if it's uint8, or [-128, 127] if it's int8. For (x / y_scale), it's rounding to the nearest even
